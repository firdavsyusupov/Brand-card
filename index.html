CocaCola

Coca-Cola is one of the most well-known and widely consumed carbonated beverages in the world. It was created in 1886 and has a distinctive cola flavor with hints of spices and orange. Coca-Cola is available in various variations, including diet and zero-calorie versions.

Fanta

Fanta is another popular carbonated beverage produced by The Coca-Cola Company. It is a fruit-flavored soda available in different flavors such as orange, lemon, tangerine, and others. Fanta is popular among those who enjoy refreshing fruit-flavored drinks.

Sprite

Sprite is a carbonated beverage created by The Coca-Cola Company, featuring a lemon-lime flavor. It is known for its refreshing taste and thirst-quenching qualities. Sprite is also available in various variations, including diet and zero-calorie versions.
